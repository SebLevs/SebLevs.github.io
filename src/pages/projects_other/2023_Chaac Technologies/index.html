TECHNOLOGIES
Unity

To create its VR solutions, Chaac Technologies employ the Unity game engine.

Unity VR technology & Occulus Meta Quest II

Although I am not the employee commonly tasked with writing systems that implement controls over the VR hardware, in my time at Chaac Technologies, I have extensively worked with Unity's XR technology in relation to the Meta Quest II.

Networking

At Chaac Technologies, we use both Netcode for GameObject and Photon PUN2 to manage the networked aspects of our projects.

As one of the two main employee assigned to networking, I have mastered the ins and outs of networking for games.
Among other things, I wrote utility systems that makes implementing other systems quicker.
I also wrote a lobby and room system under PUN2 that is both scalable et reusable.

Unity Version Control

At Chaac Technologies, the de facto source control application is Unity's (previously called Plastic SCM).

I have acquired a mastery of the general usages of the application, whether it is pushing, pulling, merging, shelving, reverting an item to a previous history state or managing code reviews.

Atlassian solutions

Jira and Confluence are both extensively used to manage the various projects produced at Chaac Technologies.

Where Jira is used to manage sprints and tasks, Confluence is used to create any necessary documentation and build test pages to log bug reports by any members of the teams, whenever required.
